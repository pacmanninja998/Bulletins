Getting Started

View Bulletins

  • Go to the View Bulletins page to view bulletins obviously
  • You have filter, to help search...
  • Click a bulletin to view more details
  • You can create a prefered job list
    1. Hit the little check box on a job
    2. On Mobile hit the arrow on the right of the screen, desktop just look to the right of the screen
    3. To close on Mobile hit the arrow on the left of the screen, desktop just direct your gaze to the left
    4. With multiple jobs selected, you can drag and rearrange them
    5. Once done you can go down the list clicking each job and the bulletin number will conveniently be copied and ready to be pasted into crewcall. You're fucking welcome.
    6. Hate a job you accidently added? Tough shit, either find it on the main list and uncheck it or skip it.. It's not that hard...

Edit Bulletins

  1. Go to the Edit Bulletins page to add new bulletins obviously
  2. Paste the bulletin text and enter the bulletin number
  3. Select whether it's for Engineer or Conductor position
  4. Review the automatically extracted information
  5. Save the bulletin to the system
